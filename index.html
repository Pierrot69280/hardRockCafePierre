WORLD BURGER CONTEST 2019

Since 30 October 2016, Hard Rock Cafe has been packing its bags in the capital of gastronomy. Lyon, for Hard Rock Cafe,
is a mix of cultures, an extraordinary architecture, a lively city and above all, a region that has a lot to offer. Especially on
our plates. Here, American cuisine and its mythical burgers meet fresh and local Lyon products. The duo is a success; the
Hard Rock Cafe Lyon wins the prestigious title of "Best Burger in the World" at the Sirah World Burger Contest 2019.
Discover or rediscover the SIRHA'S WORLD BEST BURGER at the menu at your Hard Rock Cafe Lyon. Enough to make
your taste buds wriggle.

WHEN LOCAL AND INTERNATIONAL MEET

As you can see, between local and international, the feeling passes at Hard Rock Cafe Lyon. Our iconic menu is always
present, while adapting to the Lyon landscape. Find your favourite Legendary Burger or our unmistakable cocktails and
discover our new variations. Who says no to a French toast Praluline? Not forgetting of course our meticulous selection of
French wines and our Cuvée Hard Rock Cafe beer.

800 SQUARE METRES OF SPACE

Hard Rock Cafe Lyon, it's 800 m square of space to lounge in front of your Steak Salad, or to privatise and make your
events 100% rock. In addition to an imposing room overlooking our open kitchen, enjoy an outdoor terrace, two bars, a
tasting cellar and of course our legendary Rock Shop. Don't leave without your official souvenir Hard Rock Cafe Lyon. Our
concert hall hosts shows all year round that you won't soon forget. Follow us on our various social networks to be
informed of the next dates to come.
